## McKinsey

### McKinsey — State of AI 2025
- **Source:** [The State of AI in 2025: Agents, Innovation, and Transformation](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai)
- **Survey:** June 25 – July 29, 2025. 1,993 participants across 105 nations.
- **Claims used:**
  - **88%** of respondents report regular AI use in at least one business function (up from 78% prior year) — ✅ Verified
  - **39%** of respondents attribute any level of EBIT impact to AI (most say <5% of EBIT) — ✅ Verified

### McKinsey — Unlocking Success in Digital Transformations
- **Source:** [Unlocking Success in Digital Transformations (PDF)](https://www.mckinsey.com/~/media/McKinsey/Business%20Functions/Organization/Our%20Insights/Unlocking%20success%20in%20digital%20transformations/Unlocking-success-in-digital-transformations.pdf)
- **Claim used:**
  - **Only 16%** of digital transformations fully succeed (improved performance AND sustained changes long-term). Even in digitally savvy industries (high tech, media, telecom), success ≤26%. — ✅ Verified

### McKinsey / WEF — Global Lighthouse Network 2025
- **Source:** [Global Lighthouse Network 2025 Press Release](https://www.weforum.org/press/2025/01/global-lighthouse-network-2025-world-economic-forum-recognizes-companies-transforming-manufacturing-through-innovation/)
- **Source:** [Evolution of the Global Lighthouse Network — McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/operations/our-insights/the-continuing-evolution-of-the-global-lighthouse-network)
- **Claims used:**
  - **53%** average boost in labour productivity among Lighthouse factories — ✅ Verified
  - **26%** reduction in conversion costs — ✅ Verified
  - Network expanded to 189 production facilities (Jan 2025) — ✅ Verified

### McKinsey — Digital Twin Adoption
- **Source:** [Product Digital Twins — McKinsey Operations](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/operations/how-we-help-clients/product-development-procurement/product-digital-twins)
- **Claim used:**
  - **~75%** of companies in advanced industries have adopted digital-twin technologies at medium+ complexity — ✅ Verified
